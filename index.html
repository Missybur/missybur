  • Thermalgram... It's getting warm in here
    Missy Bur ● Jun 26, 2015

    For this hackathon we used Thermal technology made by FLIR to make “Thermalgram” an application similar to Instagram, which you can upload “Thelfies(Thermal Selfies)” and people can rate on a scale of 1 - 5 how “hot” they think your picture is and take into account your actual body temperate to give you an average rating through an algorithm that our team created. In order to complete this project I used HTML, CSS, mySQL, Javascript, JQuery, Python, Sublime, Git-Hub. Check out the app on thermalgram.com and look out for it on the Android store sometime soon!
