SafeWays

Missy Bur ● July 26, 2015

Traveling alone can be a risky business, especially as a women. Being aware of this, the SafeWays team has decided to create a mobile application that would enable women, or any individual interested in the service, to find the safest route from location A to B. How we plan to do this is through scrapping data from various available datasets online that have been published by the local police.
